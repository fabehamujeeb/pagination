Owner

Instagram was co-founded by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger in 2010. Kevin Systrom, a skilled engineer and entrepreneur, played a pivotal role in shaping the platforms vision. With a background in computer programming and a keen eye for design, he aimed to create a simple yet powerful way for people to share their lives through photos. Under Systroms leadership, Instagram quickly became one of the most popular social media platforms worldwide, revolutionizing how users communicate and connect through visual storytelling. His passion for innovation and user experience has significantly influenced the social media landscape, making Instagram a cornerstone of modern digital interaction. In 2012, Instagram was acquired by Facebook, further expanding its reach and capabilities while Systrom continued to inspire creativity and community on the platform.
